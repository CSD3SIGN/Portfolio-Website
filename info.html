World Population

The Brief

This module got me to research world population. Including the current number of people on the planet, amount of people living in certain countries, urban percentages of countires, average ages etc. The idea for this project was to turn all this information into an infographic.

The Process

I started this project just like my previous projects, on paper. First of all drawing my ideas on people and recording my research and then moving on to developing on screen.
